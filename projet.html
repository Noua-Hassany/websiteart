Leonardo da Vinci

Leonardo da Vinci was born on April 15, 1452,
in Italy and passed away on May 2, 1519.

His famous paintings include "Mona Lisa," "The Last Supper," and "Vitruvian Man."

Vincent van Gogh

Vincent van Gogh was born on March 30, 1853,
in Zundert, Netherlands, and passed away on July 29, 1890.
His famous paintings include "Starry Night," "Sunflowers," and "The Bedroom."
